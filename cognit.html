Certification

Noah Tech is an officially certified MoCA member. Certification supports mastery-level proficiency that benefits raters, the test, and patients.

As a Certified MoCA member, Noah Tech has official access to updated MoCA versions and the latest digital MoCA screening tools. MoCA is a screening tool used by healthcare professionals as a first step to detect evidence of cognitive impairment. With proper administration and scoring, MoCA results accurately reflect cognitive performance. As a screening tool, MoCA is not designed to diagnose a specific condition or disease.

Why MoCA?

MoCA is a brief, 30-question test that helps healthcare professionals detect cognitive impairment very early, enabling faster diagnosis and care. It is highly sensitive for detecting early Alzheimer's disease, measuring executive function and multiple cognitive domains not assessed by the MMSE.
